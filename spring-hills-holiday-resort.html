Centurion Spring Hills Holiday Resort

A fine garden Three Star Resort located at Mukaiwadi Pirangut, Pune, on the way to Lavasa. Close to the Pirangut MIDC, 10 kms from the main junction called Chandini Chowk, 25 kms from the Pune Railway Station & 33 kms from the Lohegaon Airport of Pune.

Accommodations

26 Elegantly appointed rooms equipped with all the modern facilities like Direct Dialing of STD & ISD from Room, Cable TV, wi-fi Internet Ready rooms & more to fulfill the needs of the guest.

Conference & Banquets

EXPLORATION : Conference Hall complimented with wide spectrum of facilities includes business aids; audio video equipment & can accommodate up to 75 x in theatre style.

KHABANAS : Individual privacy cabins for families or groups to make romantic evenings more romantic.

MOONLIGHT : Open air Roof Top area, for oh so romantic times!, Capacity of upto 80 pax.

WHISPERING WINDS : Swimming Pool Side area for making the memorable occasions memorable. can accommodate 70 persons.

INDAI LAWNS : Can accommodate up to 5000 pax, best for receptions - marriages, corporate events, concerts, sporting events etc.

GAMING FACILITIES : Cricket, Badminton, Cricket, Tennis, Chess, Tambola, Foot ball, Caroms, and Playing Cards etc.

Recreation Center

"O" KIT : The Lounge Bar with a dance floor.

GARDENIA : A well laid out elegant, Multi cuisine Restaurant, Serving Continental, Indian, Chinese, Mughlai Delicacies, Open from breakfast till inner (7 AM to 11 PM).

Services

Swimming pool, 24 hrs in room dining, Chargeable Pick up & Drop from/to station or airport, Banqueting & Conferencing Facilities, Visa and Master Cards are accepted, Doctor on Call, Laundry facility etc.

Activities at the Resort

Nature Walk, Billiards, Badminton, Foot Ball, Basket Ball, Cricket, Carom, Chess, Playing Cards, Ample Car Parking, Conference Hall, Coffee Shop & dinner with Disco.
