Overview

JenJon Holiday Homes is located in Lavasa. Here the travellers can stay in managed apartments / villas. Thus the Holiday Home provides good value-for-money accommodation, a unique as well as memorable experience.

Keeping in mind that Lavasa is a beautiful planned hill city, JenJon Holiday Homes are designed to give you the real feel of Lavasa. Everyone loves a larger than life experience; therefore, all the homes are built as large and well - furnished apartments and villas. To make your holiday memorable and worth it, all the properties of JenJon have a view of lake along with a balcony, where you, your family and friends can sit and enjoy incredible natural beauty of Lavasa.

JenJon Holiday Homes has implemented a rigorous quality inspection system to guarantee that the affiliated homes provide services and amenities that meet the expectations of the discerning traveller.

When you come to Lavasa you should not bother yourself with small things but should set yourself free to the abundances of the nature and the beautiful city. You should pamper yourself with more comfort, leisure, fun and spend quality time with your family.

JenJon Holiday Homes offers you precisely that, as they work with individual owners to organise the growing Home Stays industry in Lavasa and market it under a strong brand.

Complimentary Starter Pack

When you arrive at the apartment / villa - a complimentary:
Refreshment pack consisting of water, tea, coffee, milk and sugar is provided. Starter pack of basic household items that includes a small supply of soap powder, washing up liquid. toilet rolls, dish cloths and mosquito repellent. As these are exhausted you will need to purchase more.

Personal Supervision

House Managers, each have their own properties to look after and will prepare your accommodation for your arrival and keep a keen eye on things during your holiday. To ensure the smooth running of the property and your stay is as comfortable as possible, a report form in each villa is provided where in you can record anything that needs attention.

Cleaning Service

Each property has a cleaning service once in two days of your stay.
The cleaner's daily duties include:
Cleaning bathrooms, bed making, sweeping, dusting, washing pots and pans, cleaning the kitchen.

Not included:

Food preparation or cooking
Shopping, ironing of personal laundry items

Extra Mattresses

On each apartment / villa description the guests will get the maximum occupancy possible within the configuration of the beds, bedrooms and bathrooms. However, if your party requires additional sleeping accommodation then the guest may enquire about for an additional sleeping arrangements. There is a charge of Rs.750/- per day per extra bed. Bed linen and towels are included.

Local taxes, Gas and Electricity

There are no hidden charges. The price of your holiday rental includes all local taxes, gas and electricity charges.

Bed Linen and Towels

All bed linen and bathroom towels are supplied and laundered after 3 days of your stay

Arrival and Departure

Property Briefing
On arrival or shortly after, a member of the JenJon Holidays team will meet you at your property where you will receive a short briefing on how the house works.

Departure Meeting

At a pre-arranged time during the last day of your holiday, a member of the team will call to see you to reconfirm your return arrangements, note any breakages and clear any charges.
