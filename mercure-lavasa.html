Hotel Mercure Lavasa

Hotel Overview

Mercure Lavasa is located in a scenic valley at Warasgaon Lake and surrounded by mountains. Easily accessible and positioned 2km from the Lavasa International Convention Centre, the hotel sits 80 minutes from Pune and features130 modern rooms, a restaurant and bar. Lavasa, a picturesque planned city, is the largest hill station in India. Its many attractions include a temperate climate, abundant open space and world class educational institutions and recreational activities.

Driving from Pune City, head towards bund garden crossing Yerwada. After that you will cross Sanchatai hospital in Shivaji Nagar. Continue towards Agriculture Square and towards Pashan Road and turn right towards Chandni Chowk toll plaza. The ghat area to Lavasa begins here and landmarks en route include the Indo German Engineering Institute - Technology Park and Temghar Dam. When you reach Lavasa Gate, drive through and proceed through the ghat area. The hotel is located on the right past the Town Hall.

Mercure Lavasa is just 10 minutes of walking distance from one of the finest convention centres of India, the Lavasa International Convention Centre. Equipped with one main hall divisible into eight smaller halls and six additional breakaway rooms to hold simultaneous sessions LICC is an ideal venue to host conferences for 1000 delegates, cocktail & dinner for 1500 guests, corporate party for 500 or smaller board meetings for a group of 15.

Mercure Lavasa with personalized international hospitality and home like experience adds another leaf in one of the world largest brand of midscale hotels.

Through the window from every room is all together a different world, offering never ending view of the lake front and a unique eco system which is intact. Based on principles of new urbanism, and located in a cosmopolitan city of Pune, the township is master planned with the objective of striking a balance between international architecture and nature friendly settings.

Stay at Mercure Lavasa is unified with essentials in relaxed living and greenery expected from a leisure destination, all planned to satisfy the senses. The 130 rooms are fully air conditioned and equipped with all amenities chosen to give a homelike feel on every visit.

About Lavasa

Come experience a whole new vision of living at the Hill City of Lavasa. The first city in India to be built under a Public-Private partnership, Lavasa offers the best of urban amenities, leisure activities and opportunities in an eco-enhanced environment. The Hill City is situated amidst the Sahyadri Mountains, along the shores of Warasgaon Lake. A fourth of the size of Mumbai, the city is leading the New Urbanism movement in India. It offers a dynamic swirl of opposites. Here tranquility is never far from a cosmopolitan lifestyle, technology co-exists with nature and spiritual experience complements an enterprising spirit. Life at Lavasa is truly life in full.
