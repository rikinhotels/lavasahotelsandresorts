Hotels in Lavasa

Lavasa is located in the western mountain ranges of India, Lavasa is very near to the two major metropolises, Mumbai and Pune.

Hotels & Resorts in Lavasa

A haven for tourists as well as its residents, Lavasa is home to many international hotels which suit every pocket. A getaway from the buzz of city life, corporate and business houses can hold important meetings in a setting that is so close to nature.

Currently there are three hotels operational in Lavasa as under

Fortune Select Dasve
 Spread over 1.5 acres, Fortune Select Dasve has 60 well appointed rooms including 48 Standard Rooms, 06 Executive Suites and 06 Suites
The Waterfront Shaw Apartment Hotel
 The Waterfront Shaw Apartment Hotel offers 40 serviced apartments. A blend of studio, 1BHK and 2BHK apartments with a new F&B concept, "American Diner"
Mercure LAVASA
 Mercure LAVASA
Mercure offering luxury hotel comfort at an affordable price.
Mercure Lavasa Offers 130 rooms including 96 Standard rooms, 31 Superior rooms, 2 Family rooms, 1Accessibility room, Broadband connectivity in all rooms, Wi-Fi connectivity in all public areas, Flat screen LCD televisions in all rooms, Mini-bar, Tea/Coffee station.
JenJon Holiday Homes
 JenJon Holiday Homes at Lavasa
JenJon Holiday Homes provide Studio, 1BHK, 2BHK, 3BHK Apartments and Villas with all facilities like DTH connections, car parking, extra mattress, Kitchenette with tea or coffee maker, refrigerator, JenJon also has the finest Restaurants and Cafe House in Lavasa providing delicious veg. and non veg. meals
Spring Hills Holiday Resort
 Spring Hills Holiday Resort - 30 kms from Lavasa offers 26 Elegantly appointed rooms equipped with all the modern facilities like Direct Dialing of STD & ISD from Room, Cable TV, wi-fi Internet Ready rooms &more to fulfill the needs of the guest.
