Lavasa is located in the western mountain ranges of India, Lavasa is very near to the two major metropolises, Mumbai and Pune.

The Waterfront Shaw Apartment Hotel

(Lavasa Corporation) At Village-Dasve

The Waterfront Shaw, Lavasa, (Lavasa Corporation) At Village-Dasve, Post Pathershet, Taluka-Mulshi, Lavasa, Pune.

Nestled amidst the pristine surroundings of the Sahyadri hills, The Waterfront Shaw, Lavasa is an apartment hotel which caters to business as well as leisure travelers. The apartment offers one bedroom, two bedroom and studio apartments with a breathtaking lake view. Well furnished apartments fitted with modern amenities and warm staff will ensure that you have an unforgettable and pleasant experience.

The Water Front Shaw Apartment Hotel

Choose to stay in comfort
The Waterfront Shaw gives you a choice of Studio, 1 Bedroom and 2 Bedroom apartments with a breathtaking lake-side view to choose from. Each apartment has been styled and fully furnished to give you a feel of modernity and comfort. The amenities include a fully equipped kitchenette that lets you cook and dine from Day 1. The Wi-Fi connectivity, extending throughout the hotel, further enhances your feel of modernity at The Waterfront Shaw.

Your home for a week or longer
You may be looking to break away from your hectic schedule for a week or two. You may be a professional from Pune looking for accommodation that’s close to work yet removed from the city grind. You may be a writer, composer or artist seeking tranquility to get inspired. No matter what your are looking for, The Waterfront Shaw is just the place for you. Our well appointed apartments have all the facilities and amenities to make your stay, ranging from a week to a year, pleasant and memorable.

Journey down the lake
We have a fleet of row as well as pedal boats to get you right in the middle of Lake Warasgaon. The 20 kilometer stretch with dainty buildings at the base and majestic mountains towering above will definitely be an experience of a lifetime. Lose your self amidst nature but still stay connected with Wi-Fi.

Accommodations

The Waterfront Shaw has 43 apartments with a choice of Studio, 1 Bedroom and 2 bedroom apartments. Each apartment offer a breathtaking view. All apartments are air-conditioned and are equipped with a complete kitchenette facility apart from room safes. electronic locks and WI-FI.

The Waterfront Shaw also has something for professional, businessmen and entrepreneurs. A choice of conference rooms of varying seating capacities, each equipped with state-of-the-art facilities.
